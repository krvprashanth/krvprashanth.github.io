Here’s a list of some of the talks that I’ve given so far.

- **Meet Beagle Flap Bird-like Flapping Wing Robot runs ROS** at RosCon India 2023 (slides | [video](https://youtu.be/0khPCxawC3o?si=GwTYpEgYLbuyFi5y)): In this talk, I present Beagle Flap, A flapping wing aerial robot with Linux-based AutoPilot to allow experimentation and development of control and navigation algorithms for autonomous outdoor flight operations. First, we briefly discuss the hardware platform and the current ArduPilot with MAVROS integration. Second, we explain our development of Beagle Flap and the design of a custom add-on board for PocketBeagle and DroneForce, a framework to ship custom controllers (offboard) with Ardupilot and MAVROS. Finally, we present how Open Hardware projects such as BeagleBoard.org can be used for Do-it-yourself Designs for Robotics Research.
