Academic work from my days at [Robotics Research Center](https://robotics.iiit.ac.in/), IIIT Hyderabad. Feel free to get in touch with me about this if you have any queries or would like to know more about it.

Worked on the topic of **Bio-Inspired Aerial Robotics** under the guidance of [Prof. Harikumar Kandath](https://sites.google.com/view/harikumar-kandath/home) and also a system administrator in the lab setting up robotics software and administration of 8 Linux servers for the execution of robotics simulations, hosting datasets, user and system management.
